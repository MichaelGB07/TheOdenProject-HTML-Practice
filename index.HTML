One Piece is the best anime/manga of all time. This is because of its incredible plot, interesting characters and constantly unraveling mysteries that keeps the audience engaged.

One Piece follows the adventure of Monkey D. Luffy, a 19 year old who wields the power of a devil fruit, which grants him rubber abilities while also taking away his ability to swim. His goal is to form a crew and to become king of the pirates.
